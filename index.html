Welcome to all of our brew buddies out there!!

A Little Bit About Us:

Thank you for checking out the Whiskey Row Brew Club. We are a bunch of flavor loving fermentation enthusiast. Every third Thursday we get together to discuss all things beer and more.

But actions always speak louder than words. A couple of times per year we organize events such as club brew days or horse shoes and homebrews where we meet up outdoors to take advantage of Arizona sun shine, sample each others home crafted libations, throw some horse shoes, barbeque and/or just hang out for a great time with good friends.

So if your interested in good times, go ahead and show us by clicking the button below.
